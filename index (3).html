Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do digital downloads work? +

After completing your purchase on Etsy, you'll receive an email with a link to download your files. You can also access your downloads any time from your Etsy account under "Purchases and Reviews." No shipping needed — it's instant!

Will I receive a physical item? +

No, all products in shop4ease1 are digital downloads only. Nothing will be shipped to you. You'll receive high-quality digital files (PDF, PNG, or XLSX) that you can print at home or use on your devices.

Can I print the files at home? +

Absolutely! All printables are formatted for standard home printers. We recommend printing on standard letter (8.5×11") or A4 paper. For best results, use a laser printer or inkjet with high-quality settings.

Where do I access my Etsy download? +

Log into your Etsy account and go to "You → Purchases and Reviews." Click on the order, then click "Download Files." You can download your files there as many times as you need.

Can I share or resell the files? +

Files are for personal use only and may not be resold, redistributed, or used for commercial purposes. Each purchase is licensed to a single buyer. Please see the shop's full terms on Etsy for details.
